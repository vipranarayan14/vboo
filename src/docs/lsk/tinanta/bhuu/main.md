# //bhuu//

> //bhuu sattaayaam|//

The //dhaatu//, '//bhuu//' is used in the sense of 'to be; to exist'.

The derivations of the various [conjugations](#/lsk/glossary) of the //bhuu-dhaatu// can be found by the following the links.
