rAmEbhyaH

chaturthI-vibhakti-bahuvachana-vivakShAyAm i.e. when we want to use the word, rAma in the sense of recipient or purpose (chaturthI-vibhakti) and in singular (bahuvachana), we add the subanta-pratyaya associated with same vibhakti and vachana, which is 'bhyas', to it.

rAma + bhyas

Now, the sUtra,

bahuvachanE jhalyEt| 7/3/103

says that the last letter of the a~Nga of a subanta-pratyaya is replaced by the letter, 'E' if

  1. the last letter of the a~Nga is 'a'
  2. and the first letter of subanta-pratyaya is is one the letters of the pratyAhAra, 'jhal'
  3. and the subanta-pratyaya represents bahuvachana|

Here,

  • rAma is the a~Nga of the subanta-pratyaya, 'bhyas'
  • and the last letter of the a~Nga, 'rAma' is 'a'
  • and the first letter of the subanta-pratyaya, 'bhyas' is 'bh' and this, 'bh' is one of the letters in the pratyAhAra, 'jhal'
  • and the subanta-pratyaya, 'bhyas' represents bahuvachana

So, the last letter 'a' in 'rAma' is replaced by 'E' --

rAm(a) + bhyas

= rAmE + bhyas

= rAmEbhyas

And now, by the sUtra-s, sasajuShO ruH and kharavasAnayOH... the 's' in 'rAmEbhyas' is replaced by visarga (:). [See under rAmaH for more details.] So, we get --

rAmEbhyaH

Thus, we derive the pada, 'rAmEbhyaH' by adding the subanta-pratyaya, 'bhyas' to the prAtipadika, 'rAma'|
