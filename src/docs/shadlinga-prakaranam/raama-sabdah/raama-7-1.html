saptamI-vibhakti-Ekavachana-vivakShAyAm i.e. when we want to use the word, rAma in the sense of location (saptamI-vibhakti) and in singular (Ekavachana), we add the subanta-pratyaya associated with same vibhakti and vachana, which is '~Ni', to it.

rAma + ~Ni

Now, by the sUtra --

lashakvataddhitE| 1/3/8

the letter, '~N' in '~Ni' gets the 'it'-saMj~nA and thus, disappears. So, now we have --

rAma + i

Now, the last letter of the prAtipadika and the remaining letter of the '~Ni'-pratyaya undergoes guNa-sandhi --

rAm (a + i)

= rAm (E)

= rAmE

So, finally we get --

rAmE

Thus we derive the pada, 'rAmE' by adding the subanta-pratyaya, '~Ni' to the prAtipadika, 'rAma'|
