rAmau

dvitIyA-vibhakti-dvivachana-vivakShAyAm i.e. when we want to use the word, rAma in the sense of object (dvitIyA-vibhakti) and in dual (dvivachana), we add the subanta-pratyaya associated with same vibhakti and vachana, which is 'auT', to it.

Here we have to recall that though we are talking about two rAma-s only one rAma word is taken here, following the sUtra, sarUpANAmEkashESha Ekavibhaktau|

rAma + auT

Here, by the sUtra --

halantyam| 1/3/3

the last consonant (vya~njana) in the upadEsha (here, the 'auT' pratyaya) gets the label (saMj~nA) 'it'| And by the sUtra --

tasya lOpaH| 1/3/9

the it-saMj~naka i.e. one which is labeled as 'it' gets 'lOpa' i.e. it should disappears. So, we will get --

rAma + au

Now, the rest of the prakriyA (derivation) is like that of rAmau in prathamA-vibhakti-dvivachana|
