rAmasya

ShaShTI-vibhakti-Ekavachana-vivakShAyAm i.e. when we want to use the word, rAma in the sense of any other relation to the kriyApada (ShaShTI-vibhakti) and in singular (Ekavachana), we add the subanta-pratyaya associated with same vibhakti and vachana, which is '~Nas', to it.

rAma + ~Nas

Unlike the other prakriyA-s, the prakriyA of rAmasya is very simple.

Now, by the sUtra --

TA~Nasi~NasaminAtsyAH| 7/1/12

the ShaShTI-Ekavachana-pratyaya, '~Nas' is replaced by 'sya' [Refer the prakriyA of rAmENa ].

So, now we have --

rAma + sya

Then, the prAtipadika, 'rAma' and 'sya' simply join without undergoing any change. So, we get --

rAmasya

Thus, we derive the pada, 'rAmasya' by adding the subanta-pratyaya, '~Nas' to the prAtipadika, 'rAma'|
